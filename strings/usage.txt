Usage: @1.1.1.1 <domain> [...types]

Examples:
  @1.1.1.1 mattcowley.co.uk
  @1.1.1.1 mattcowley.co.uk A AAAA

Types:
  If not provided, the default type of "A" will be used
  You can provide a type of "*" to lookup all supported types

Supported types:
  A
  NS
  CNAME
  MX
  TXT
  AAAA
  SRV
  CAA
